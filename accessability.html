Accessibility Tips

Simple ways to create more inclusive, accessible, and supportive environments for everyone.

Why Accessibility Matters

Accessibility ensures that individuals of all abilities can fully participate in everyday life. By making thoughtful adjustments, we can create environments that are more inclusive, supportive, and empowering for everyone.

Everyday Accessibility Tips

  • Keep walkways clear and free of obstacles
  • Use clear signage with easy-to-read fonts
  • Provide seating options in public spaces
  • Be patient and offer assistance when needed
  • Respect personal independence and boundaries

Readable Text

Use simple fonts, large text sizes, and high contrast colors to improve readability.

Alt Text

Add descriptions to images so screen readers can describe them to users.

Keyboard Navigation

Ensure websites can be navigated without a mouse for users with mobility challenges.

Communication Tips

Inclusive communication helps create respectful and supportive interactions. Here are a few ways to communicate effectively:

  • Speak clearly and directly
  • Listen actively and be patient
  • Ask before offering help
  • Use respectful and inclusive language
  • Avoid making assumptions

Creating Inclusive Communities

Accessibility is a shared responsibility. By making small, intentional changes in our homes, schools, workplaces, and communities, we can help create spaces where everyone feels included and supported.
