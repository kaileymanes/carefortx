Get Involved

Be part of a movement that promotes independence, accessibility, and inclusion across East Texas.

Make a Difference

Supporting independent living doesn’t always require big actions — small efforts can create meaningful change. Whether you want to volunteer, share resources, or raise awareness, there are many ways to get involved.

Volunteer

Offer your time to local organizations that support individuals with disabilities and promote independent living.

Share Resources

Help others by sharing helpful information, services, and opportunities within your community.

Raise Awareness

Use your voice to educate others about accessibility, inclusion, and the importance of independent living.

Support Your Community

Local organizations play a key role in supporting independence. By getting involved, you help strengthen the network of support that individuals rely on every day. Community involvement creates more opportunities for inclusion and growth.

Simple Ways to Help

  • Volunteer at local advocacy or support organizations
  • Donate resources or supplies (if applicable)
  • Share educational content on social media
  • Encourage inclusive practices in your school or workplace
  • Support businesses and programs that promote accessibility

Every Action Matters

Creating a more inclusive and accessible community starts with awareness and action. No effort is too small — every step contributes to a more supportive and independent future for all.
