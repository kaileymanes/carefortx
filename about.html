About Care For Texas

Care For Texas is dedicated to promoting independence, accessibility, and opportunity for individuals with disabilities across East Texas.

Our Mission

Care For Texas was created to raise awareness about the importance of independent living and to connect individuals with meaningful resources. We aim to support dignity, inclusion, and equal opportunity by providing accessible information and highlighting real experiences.

Why It Matters

Independent living is about more than just access — it’s about having the freedom to make choices, participate in the community, and live with dignity. Many individuals still face barriers in housing, employment, and accessibility, which is why education and advocacy are so important.

Supporting Organizations

We proudly highlight organizations such as the East Texas Center for Independent Living, which provides essential services, advocacy, and support to help individuals live independently and confidently within their communities.

Accessibility

Dignity

Supporting the right of every individual to live with respect and independence.

Community
